About Me

Hello! I am Dr. Ye Win Thein [cite: 1], a dedicated Medical Doctor and Public Health Officer currently based in Manila, Philippines [cite: 4]. I have 9 years of professional experience specializing in Tuberculosis prevention, care, and control [cite: 7].

I am currently a Doctor of Public Health (Epidemiology) candidate at the University of the Philippines Manila [cite: 11, 12], where I also serve as a Research Assistant at the College of Public Health [cite: 22, 23].
